What is Sudoku ?

Sudoku is a popular puzzle game in which missing numbers are to be filled into a 9 by 9 grid of squares which are subdivided into 3 by 3 boxes so that every row, every column, and every box contains the numbers 1 through 9. As mentioned above there are 3 conditions that need to be met to successfully complete the puzzle:

  • Complete all the rows of the puzzle with the numbers 1 to 9 without repetition
  • Complete all the columns of the puzzle with the numbers 1 to 9 without repetition
  • Complete all the 3 by 3 boxes of the puzzle with the numbers 1 to 9 without repetition

N/B: Sudoku is not only limited to numbers some puzzles are made up of other type of objects, but the rules remain same
